About Sweet & Savory

Where tradition meets innovation in every bite

Our Story

Founded in 2018, Sweet & Savory began as a passion project to bring authentic European baking traditions to our local community. We believe that the best baked goods start with the finest ingredients, time-honored techniques, and a genuine love for the craft.

Every morning before sunrise, our bakers arrive to begin the daily ritual of mixing, shaping, and baking. From our signature sourdough starter that's been lovingly maintained for over five years to our delicate French pastries, each item is crafted with meticulous attention to detail.

What Makes Us Special

  • Fresh daily baking using traditional European techniques
  • Premium ingredients sourced from local farms when possible
  • Custom cakes and catering for special occasions
  • Small-batch production ensuring quality over quantity
  • Family recipes passed down through generations
